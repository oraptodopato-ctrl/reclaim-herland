Archive infrastructure

Archives & sources

Archival descriptions follow ICA standards (ISAD(G), ISAAR(CPF)) and are managed in AtoM (Access to Memory). This page provides an overview of institutions consulted and data types collected.

Institutional archives

Arquivo Municipal de Lisboa (AML)

Urban planning documents, project drawings, photographic collections.

Visited

Instituto Superior de Agronomia (ISA)

Academic records, student files, faculty documentation.

Scheduled

Associação Portuguesa de Arquitetos Paisagistas (APAP)

Professional records, membership files, publications.

Scheduled

Personal & private collections

Research also draws on personal archives held by the landscape architects themselves or their families. Access is negotiated on a case-by-case basis, with written authorisation for digitisation and use.

Data types collected

Archival materials

Technical drawings, landscape plans, professional correspondence, contracts, press clippings, photographs. Digitised at ≥300 dpi (TIFF/JPEG).

Oral histories

12 semi-structured interviews in video (MP4) and audio (WAV). Full transcripts in DOCX/TXT. Access subject to informed consent level.

Access to the archival database

Archival descriptions are publicly accessible via the project's AtoM instance, hosted at NOVA/HTC. The database is updated continuously as research progresses. Descriptions follow ISAD(G) and authority records follow ISAAR(CPF).
